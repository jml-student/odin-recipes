Croquettes

The croquette is a dish originated in French gastronomy that consists of a portion of dough made of a dense sauce such as bechamel and a mince of various ingredients, which has been coated in egg and breadcrumbs, and fried in plenty of oil.

Ingredients

  • Milk
  • Butter
  • Egg
  • Bread crumbs
  • Flour
  • Oil
  • Salt
  • Serrano ham
  • Nutmeg

Steps

  1. First of all, cut the ham in small pieces and put some butter on low heat.
  2. Add the ham, cook it all a couple of minutes and add some flour.
  3. Put the milk and cook over low heat, stirring all the time.
  4. Add some salt and nutmeg, cook for another 15 to 20 minutes.
  5. Remove all the dough to a bowl and wait until it cools to make small balls with it.
  6. Once you have all the balls, coat them in bread crumbs and egg in that order.
  7. Finally fry all the balls and you got it!
